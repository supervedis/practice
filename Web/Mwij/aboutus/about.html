Dealers and consultants in: Fertilizers, Seed & Agrochemicals; Soil Fertility, Fertilizer Use, Agricultural Extension & Marketing; Draft Policy Development & Development of Strategic Plans, Monitoring and Evaluation; and Software Development tailored towards Agricultural needs.

To be the choice provider of quality, efficient and effective specialist agricultural extension and marketing services

To contribute to improved farm production through supply of agricultural inputs and provision of quality advisory services on the use of effective and appropriate strategies, approaches, farm techniques, methodologies and technologies

Background

MWIJ FarmTech Consultants is a reputable consultancy firm established to contribute to the transformation of agriculture into a commercial business enterprise leading to sustained food security, improved farm incomes and environmental conservation. It supplements efforts of Governments, non- government organisations (NGOs), development partners and other private practitioners through contractual partnerships.

The firm is very strong in the provision of fertilizer extension & marketing services and soft ware development, which are the major ingredients for commercial agriculture. It applies a Value Chain Approach (VCA) in which market demand informs what to produce, where to produce, when to produce, for whom to produce and how to produce. We conduct contracted feasibility studies and review of technical papers in our specialist areas and also carry out monitoring & evaluation of projects and programmes. The firm also supplies affordable fertilizers, seed and agrochemicals, which are commensurate with the services offered.

Objectives


Impact Indicators

To contribute to increased agricultural production, farm productivity, food security and farm incomes for improved livelihoods of rural communities through their access to affordable advisory services on appropriate and sustainable farm techniques and technologies.

Outcome indicators

Effective and sustainable farm techniques and technologies made available to demanding clientele institutions and individuals.

Output Indicators

  • To supply fertilizers, seed and agrochemicals appropriate in designated localities
  • To provide consultancy services on techniques in soil fertility management and fertilizer use options
  • To provide consultancy services in marketing techniques, marketing intelligence and information service
  • To provide consultancy services in project development and monitoring & evaluation
  • To offer advisory services in draft policy development and development strategies
  • To develop appropriate software programmes/models on demand
