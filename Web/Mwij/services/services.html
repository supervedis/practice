Soil Management

Soil is the natural resource that provides anchorage and nutrition for plants. Soil fertility is the state of the soil when it contains plant nutrients in amounts, balanced proportions and forms available for absorption by plants. Soil Management involves the build-up, restoration and maintenance of soil fertility, achieved through 1) soil conservation structures and 2) soil fertility management practices. We provide services on soil conservation structures and soil fertility management practices.

Fertilizer Use

Fertilizer use is a practice of soil fertility management. Fertilizer carries food for plants known as plant nutrients. Fertilizer types contain plant nutrients in varying proportions. Fertilizer enables the seed to express its potential in physical yield. It must be, judiciously, used for the crop to attain its yield potential. Research has shown that 50% of increase in grain yield can be attributed to efficient use of fertilizers. We provide fertilizer extension services for farmers and related institutions to achieve fertilizer use efficiency (FUE). FUE means right type, correct rates, right time and correct mode of fertilizer application.

Agricultural Extension

We translate agricultural research findings into extension messages for ease of farmers’ adoption. We use extension strategies, approaches, methodologies and techniques that enable farmers to access appropriate extension messages on crop and livestock production. Extension strategies are the means we employ to achieve the targets. Extension approaches are the different styles we use to provide extension services. Extension methodologies are processes through which our messages are transmitted while extension techniques are the actual methods or tools of delivering extension packages.

Agricultural Marketing

Agriculture must be commercial for the country to attain self-reliance in farm produce, food security and farm incomes. The market must determine and inform on what to produce, why produce, when to produce, for whom to produce, and how to produce and distribute. We provide marketing services in agricultural value chains (AVCs) for various enterprises. We use AVC approach to link farmers to markets. This is to ensure that available markets determine what to produce and how much to produce. This is the pre-requisite for transformation to a commercially competitive agriculture. We analyse market functions in (AVCs) and recommend the various marketing models appropriate to situations.

Feasibility Studies

We help in transforming ideas into tangible projects. Our feasibility study reports establish viability of customers’ concepts and determine their success or otherwise. Clients can make strategic decisions on proceeding with planning and implementation of projects. We incorporate risk factors and mitigation measures against them.

Our approach on feasibility studies is holistic in that we consider social, commercial, technical, economic, market, ecological and operational perspectives of the business concept. We carry out feasibility studies in the following circumstances among others:

  • Business start-up;
  • Introducing major changes in the business;
  • Acquiring a new enterprise;
  • Purchase of an existing business;
  • Change in business location; and
  • Introducing software applications.

Monitoring and Evaluation

Smooth implementation of projects require adjustments in business plans and even a re-look into strategic plans. An in-built monitoring & evaluation system identifies flaws during implementation that inform desirable adjustments. We develop customer-specific computerised monitoring and evaluation systems. We conduct summative evaluation studies of on-going projects to inform on needed changes in the next project cycle. Indicators for the deliverables determine if the project is on course for achieving its purpose. We also conduct ex-post evaluation for projects that have been completed to measure their success in achieving the overall goals. The impact indicators are the main tools that inform the success or shortcomings of projects towards achieving the overall goals. Our Evaluation Reports determine progression into new ventures, re-designing or closure of projects.
